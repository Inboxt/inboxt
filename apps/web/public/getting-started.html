Thank you for joining! Inboxt helps you save, organize, and read newsletters and articles distraction-free. Here’s what you can do:

Core Features

  • Save articles and newsletters for later reading.
  • Organize everything using labels and powerful search.
  • Read anywhere — your content stays synced in a clean, reader-friendly view.
