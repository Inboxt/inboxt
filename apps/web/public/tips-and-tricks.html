Make the most of Inbox Reader with these handy tips and shortcuts.

Quick Shortcuts

  • +: Add a new article by link.
  • J/K: Move up and down your list.
  • O: Open the original article source.

Pro Organization

  • Create and assign labels as you save, or anytime from each article.
  • Use search to instantly find articles by keyword, label, or source.
  • Bulk archive by selecting multiple items with Shift or Ctrl/Cmd + click.

Saving Content

  • Forward newsletters to your personal Inbox Reader email address (find it in Settings) to save them automatically.
  • Browser extension is coming soon for one-click saves from your favorite sites.

Limitations & Support

  • 🚫 Attachments, paywalled, and some dynamic web content may not be fully supported yet.
  • 🙋‍♂️ Something’s not working? Check our help, or contact support@inbox-reader.com.
