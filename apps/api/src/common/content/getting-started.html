Welcome to Inboxt

Inboxt is a calm place for articles and newsletters you want to read later. There are no feeds, recommendations, or algorithms. What you save is what you see.

The basic idea

  1. Save articles or newsletters you care about.
  2. Read them later in a clean, focused reader.
  3. Organize your library so it stays useful over time.

Save your first item

  • Click Add link in the top bar and paste any article URL.
  • On desktop, installing the browser extension gives you one-click saving.
  • On mobile, you can share a page directly to Inboxt from your browser.

Once saved, items appear immediately and finish processing in the background.

Read without distractions

Articles and newsletters open in a reader view that removes ads, navigation, and clutter. You can adjust fonts, spacing, and theme to match how you like to read.

Select text to create highlights. Highlights sync across devices and can be exported later.

Keep things organized

  • Archive items to clear your Inbox without deleting them.
  • Labels help group related content across your library.
  • Search lets you filter by content, labels, type, site, and more.

Deleted items go to the trash and are removed automatically after 30 days.

Newsletters work too

You can create personal inbound email addresses and use them to subscribe to newsletters or forward emails you want to read later.

Newsletters appear alongside articles and use the same reader and organization tools.

A few important things

  • Verifying your email unlocks extensions, newsletters, imports, exports, and API access.
  • Your data is yours. You can export it or delete your account at any time.

Where to go next

You can start using Inboxt right away by saving a few articles.
