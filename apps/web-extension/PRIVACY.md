### Inboxt Browser Extension Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 5, 2026**

Inboxt is a privacy-first, self-hosted read-later service. This privacy policy explains how the Inboxt browser extension handles user data.

**1. Data Handling**
The Inboxt extension captures the following information only when you explicitly click the "Save" button:
*   **URL & Page Content:** The extension reads the URL and the HTML content of the active tab to save the article to your library.
*   **Authentication:** The extension stores your self-hosted instance URL and API token locally to authenticate requests to your server.

**2. Data Transmission**
All data captured by the extension is sent directly to the "App URL" you configured in the extension settings. We do not use any intermediary servers.

**3. Data Storage & Third Parties**
*   We (the developers of Inboxt) do not collect, store, or have access to any of your data.
*   Your data is stored on your own self-hosted infrastructure.
*   We do not share, sell, or trade your information with any third parties.

**4. Permissions**
The extension requires 'activeTab' and 'storage' permissions to function. These are used solely to capture the page you choose to save and to remember your settings.
